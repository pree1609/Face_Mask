About Mask It

We, a team of two, PySquad have improvised face detector to detect face-masks. In such unprecedented times, Mask It can be incorporated in systems to facilitate work in public and corporate spaces. The two detectors are integrated with flask to show the difference between the face detector and face_mask detector.

Mask It provides:

  • Covid-19 Tracker
    •
  • Pandemic News Updates
    •
  • Face-Mask Detector
    •
  • Face Detector
    •

On pressing the Face-Mask Detector, it enables webcam and shows the relevant output. Mask It only detects the surgical masks. On pressing the Face detector , it enables the webcam and recognizes the face area. The frameworks used are:

  • Flask as backend framework
    •
  • OpenCV to detect face-masks
